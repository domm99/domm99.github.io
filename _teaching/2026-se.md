Teaching tutor for the **Software Engineering** course in the Master's Degree
Programme in **Digital Transformation Management** at the University of Bologna.

The course introduces the principles, methods, and tools used to design,
develop, test, and maintain complex software systems. The teaching activities
cover core software engineering topics, including requirements analysis,
software architecture, design methodologies, development processes, testing,
and collaborative software development.

As a teaching tutor, I support students during practical activities and
laboratory sessions, assist them with exercises and project work, and provide
guidance on the application of software engineering practices and tools.

The course is worth **6 ECTS** and is offered during the first year of the
Digital Transformation Management programme at the Cesena Campus.
