Teaching tutor for the **Fundamentals of Computer Science** course offered within
the Bachelor's Degree Programmes in **Biomedical Engineering** and
**Electronics Engineering** at the University of Bologna.

The course introduces the foundations of computer science and imperative
programming, with particular emphasis on algorithmic thinking, structured
programming, and fundamental data structures.

The teaching activities cover topics such as computer architecture,
information representation, algorithms, control structures, functions,
arrays, pointers, dynamic memory allocation, strings, recursion, searching,
and sorting, using the **C programming language**.

As a teaching tutor, I support students during laboratory sessions and practical
activities, assist them with programming exercises, and help them prepare for
the written and practical examination.

The course is worth **6 ECTS** and is offered at the Cesena Campus.
