I presented our paper **“Towards Intelligent Pulverized Systems: a Modern Approach for Edge-Cloud Services”** at WOA 2024, hosted in the historic setting of Forte di Bard.

The talk explored how the pulverization of software components across the edge–cloud continuum can be combined with distributed intelligence and adaptive coordination. In particular, it discussed a vision in which computation, learning, and coordination responsibilities can be dynamically distributed across heterogeneous devices and infrastructure.

The workshop provided a stimulating environment for discussing the relationship between multi-agent systems, collective intelligence, and modern edge–cloud applications.
