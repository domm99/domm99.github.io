I presented **“Towards Self-Adaptive Cooperative Learning in Collective Systems”** at the Doctoral Symposium of ACSOS 2024.

The talk outlined the broader vision of my PhD research: engineering populations of distributed devices that can learn cooperatively, reorganize their interactions, and adapt their learning strategies as environmental conditions, data distributions, and available resources evolve.

The Doctoral Symposium was a valuable opportunity to present the overall research direction beyond a single paper and to receive feedback from researchers working on autonomic and self-organizing systems.
